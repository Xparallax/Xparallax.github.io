How to stay safe from different types of creatures

Metal is often beleieved to possess magical properties, ones which could be used against several creatures. The skin of a Changeling becomes immediatley irritated upon contact to iron. Silver deals a great amount of damage to werewolves, a silver bullet can even kill a vampire, which is half werewolf.

giraffe creature

In order to protect yourself from any alien giraffe species you must snap their long necks. Typically alot of force is needed to get thrpugh their thick veiny necks but once done they'll lose all source of their power. Its' also rumored that they are allergic to cherries, specifically dark ones, but this thesis hasn't been proven yet.

Fish species

Fish species can often be fought off by creating an ecsessive amount of dry oxygen around it. By removing the moisture in the air around it its gills would become unlubricated and unable to slide against eachother, completely blocking off any reception of air or water, killing them instantly.

Wereworlf

A werewolf has long been thought to be fought off strictly with silver, but a recnt school of thought has suggested a new weapon against them; corn. Peeling off each individual piece of corn from a cob and launching them at an attacking werewolf will instantly disintegrate them into particles of nitrogen sulfide and iron. These molecules should be immediatley handled and stored in an air tight chamber or they will regenerate.
